Astralcast 2.0 — Starfield Specification

Purpose

The Starfield is the heart of Astralcast.

The user is not selecting tarot cards.

The user is interacting with a living celestial field and discovering archetypes hidden among the stars.

The ritual must feel mysterious, immersive, responsive, and intentional.

---

Starfield Composition

Background Stars

Count:

- 500–1000

Behavior:

- Random twinkling
- Random brightness variation
- Decorative only
- Never selectable
- No tarot association

Purpose:

- Create the illusion of a vast cosmos

---

Oracle Stars

Count:

- 78

Behavior:

- Hidden among the background stars
- Selectable
- One Oracle Star per tarot archetype

Tarot Mapping:

- 22 Major Arcana
- 56 Minor Arcana

Total:

- 78 Tarot Archetypes

Important:

- Oracle Stars must appear visually identical to one another.
- Users should never be able to identify a card before selection.

---

Reading Initialization

When the user enters the Starfield:

1. Shuffle tarot deck
2. Assign all 78 cards to Oracle Stars
3. Randomize Oracle Star positions
4. Render starfield

Every reading creates a new sky.

---

Ritual Sequence

Step 1 — Focus

Instruction:

"Touch and hold the screen while focusing on your question."

User presses finger on screen.

---

Step 2 — Gathering

Oracle Stars begin moving toward the user's finger.

Behavior:

- Smooth movement
- Slight acceleration
- Organic motion

Purpose:

- The stars acknowledge the user's intention

---

Step 3 — Orbit

Stars begin orbiting around the user's finger.

Behavior:

- Circular motion
- Increasing speed
- Responsive to finger movement

If the finger moves:

- Orbit center follows finger

Purpose:

- Create a feeling of energetic buildup

---

Step 4 — Release

User lifts finger.

Behavior:

- Stars scatter outward
- Momentum carries stars away
- Stars gradually settle

Purpose:

- Release intention into the cosmos

---

Card Selection

After stars settle:

User taps stars.

Spread Rules:

Single Card

Selections Required:

- 1

Three Card

Selections Required:

- 3

Tree of Life

Selections Required:

- 10

Once enough stars are selected:

- Begin reveal sequence

---

Reveal Sequence

Selected star:

1. Brightens
2. Expands
3. Emits particles
4. Transforms into tarot archetype

Reveal should feel ceremonial rather than mechanical.

---

Audio

Ambient:

- Soft cosmic atmosphere
- Minimalist

Gather:

- Gentle energy swell

Orbit:

- Subtle harmonic tone

Reveal:

- Soft chime

No loud magical effects.

No jump scares.

No casino-style feedback.

---

Design Principles

The experience should feel:

- Sacred
- Calm
- Intentional
- Cosmic
- Personal

Avoid:

- Flashiness
- Excessive effects
- Obvious randomness
- Game-like behavior

The user should feel they are participating in a ritual, not playing a game.
